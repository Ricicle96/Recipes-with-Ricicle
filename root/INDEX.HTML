About me

Hey there, I'm Josh, a 29 year old amateur home cook from Derbyshire. I've been cooking now for the past 12 years, starting when I first discovered my passion for cookery in my GCSE Home Economics course. This website is a dedication to my years of learning, the recipes I've trialed, those that have succeded and those that have failed spectacularly. My hope is that this can help any number of budding new cooks out there to not make the mistakes I made.
